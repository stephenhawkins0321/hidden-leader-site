7 Morning Habits for Quiet Strength

Updated 2025-09-16

  1. Scripture First (5–10 min): A short reading and reflection. Consider a study Bible like this.
  2. Silence (3 min): Breathe, pray, and set intent.
  3. Hydrate + Salt: Water and a pinch of salt for energy.
  4. Movement (10–15 min): Push-ups, air squats, carries. Adjustable dumbbells help.
  5. Plan 3 Wins: Write the top three outcomes for today.
  6. Protein Breakfast: Keep it simple—eggs + meat + fruit. Meal prep containers like these help consistency.
  7. No Phone Until #1–#3 are done.
