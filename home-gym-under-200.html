Build a Beginner’s Home Gym for Under $200

Updated 2025-09-16

Focus on compound movements with minimal gear. Here’s a starter kit:

  • Adjustable dumbbells (core piece)
  • Resistance band set
  • Doorway pull‑up bar
  • Jump rope

Start with 3 days/week: Push/Pull/Legs. Keep notes, add reps slowly, and sleep.
